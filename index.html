About Me

I'm a recent graduate of Southern New Hampshire University earning my computer science degree in April, 2024. Proficient in C++, Java, and Python. Working on learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and C#. I'm also a musician and a former music educator. I play guitar and violin and have performed in numerous groups across New England.

I originally went to college at Keene State College as a double major in Music Education and Music Composition. I was a high school music teacher for a few years before realizing that teaching wasn't for me. During COVID, I wanted to learn how to code for fun and asked some friends to teach me. This combination of finding a new skill and losing interest in my career led me to the decision to change careers to software development. I have always loved different aspects of science. I love learning how things work so becoming a software developer made sense.

I began taking classes at Southern New Hampshire University at the beginning of 2022. I started learning Python and Java in my first few classes and I eventually learned C++ as well.

I'm currently working on my full stack web development knowledge by going through an online bootcamp course. Creating this personal site using Bootstrap was one of the projects I completed.
