Interests

When I'm not programming I'm playing/writing music, playing video games, putting together a puzzle, or watching tv.

I was about 10 years old when I started to really love music. At the time I began learning how to play the guitar from my dad and violin in school. During my teen years I played guitar in a progressive metal band with my friends and violin in our school orchestra and chamber orchestra. Since high school I have played in several ensembles and over 15 musical theater pit orchestras.

I began writing music at the end of high school and decided to add Music Composition as second major when I was at Keene State. I have composed for several sized ensembles, including string orchestra, string quartet, choir, and other various small ensembles.

One of my more recent compositions, Elemental Suite, is inspired by one of my favorite shows, Avatar: The Last Airbender. This movement is inspired by the element air.
